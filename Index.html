Our Story

A Table Rooted in the Mediterranean Coast

Aurelia began as a single wood-fired oven and a promise: to cook honestly, with ingredients that never travel farther than they need to. Today, our kitchen partners with local farms and day-boat fisheries to build a menu that shifts with the seasons — never the trends.

Every detail, from the hand-thrown ceramics to the candlelight over each table, is designed to slow you down. We're not just serving dinner. We're serving an evening.

Elena Moreau

Executive Chef

Elena Moreau

"Cooking is memory. Every dish on this menu is a place I've been, or a person I've loved."

Trained in Lyon and seasoned across kitchens in Marseille and Barcelona, Chef Elena brings two decades of coastal Mediterranean technique to Aurelia. Her philosophy is simple: source with integrity, cook with restraint, and let the ingredient speak first.
